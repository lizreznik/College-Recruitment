Let us tell you about your potential new school: Founded in 1908 as the New Jersey State Normal School at Montclair, Montclair State University is located across four New Jersey communities: Montclair, Little Falls, Clifton, and Bloomfield. The main campus covers 252 acres and offers over 300 majors, minors, concentrations, and certificate programs. With a total of 22,570 students, including 18,062 undergraduates and 4,508 graduates, Montclair State boasts a student-to-teacher ratio of 17 to 1 and an average class size of 24.
